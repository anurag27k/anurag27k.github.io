IGCAR PROJECT

Concept Simulation of Safety Related Service Water System

This was project in IGCAR(Indira Gandhi Centre for Atomic Research) Kalpakkam where I created a Concept Simulation of Safety Related Service Water System.

The coolant in the reactor(which cools down heat from Nuclear reaction) needs to be cooled by water. This water also needs to be cooled down. I have developed a concept simulation of that machine which could be used in cooling. The process involved understanding of risks and functionality specific to Nuclear reactors. I also learned about various machines and their parts and how they could be depicted, integrated and simulated in the software.
