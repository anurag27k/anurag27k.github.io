Initial intentions were that, this game could be played during the intervals betwee the IndiaHCI conference. This game could in include the content from the lectures and could also be used for teaching different languages.

This is the second game that I designed. This is an infinite jumper where the player faces various obstacles to cross. Here the player has to keep jumping and avoid the pointed obstacles and land on the flat surface. Aim of the game is to reach as high as possible.
