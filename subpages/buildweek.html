BUILD WEEK

People10 Project

Below are designs made for the event - Build Week. Here people competed each other in teams to build an application.

01

Pullup standee

Here is a standee which was designed so as to identify the event of buildweek.

This event lasted for a week where teams fought against each other to build the most useful application.

Each team was given flags and badges. They were to present their application at the end of the buildweek.

Teams were presented with prizes for best product, best pitch.
